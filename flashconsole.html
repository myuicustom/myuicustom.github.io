Flash Console

Control your flashlight as you want

About Flash Console

Flash Console: An intuitive app for flashlight control. Seamlessly toggle between rear and front flashlights, adjust intensity, and set frequencies. Essential for everyday tasks such as Video calls or any other scenario.

Features

  • Adjust frequency of blinks (Non-root)
  • Toggle front or Rear flashlight (Non-root)
  • Force toggle front or Rear flashlight (Root)
  • Use flashlight in Video calls (Root)
  • Use high intensity flashlight in Camera (Root)
  • Control Brightness of Flashlight (Root)
