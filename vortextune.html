VortexTune

Android optimizer and Dev tool

About VortexTune

VortexTune is a cutting-edge app designed to provide various app's features at a single place & unleash the full potential of your device with powerful optimization features and a sleek interface, VortexTune empowers you to take control of your device's performance and modification.

Key Features

  • System Optimization
  • ROM debloater
  • Device info
  • Reboot to bootloder option
  • Refresh rate settings
  • Rom backup
  • Clear all app's cache
  • Block Ads
  • Gaming mode
  • Option to off Google services
  • Change Selinux mode
  • Separate Wifi & mobile data (a12)
  • Extra (tap on app title for 11 times)
  • Adding more features in upcoming updates

How to Use

  1. Open and Grant root permission.
  2. Explore the various sections and features.
  3. Follow given instructions.
  4. After Running OPTIMIZE button wait for it to change from RED to WHITE again.
  5. Your Backed up partitions will be saved inside /Sdcard .
  6. Roaming button is for MYUi CN rom specific.
  7. Have any question? Feel free to ask in the support group
